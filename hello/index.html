What is the Ballarat hackerspace?

The Ballarat hackerspace is a community group for anyone to bring in any projects they are working on as a hobby, to help others or to find ways to improve your own project with a focus on electronics and gadgets. No experience is needed and it is a great place to learn and ask questions of others.

From Arduinos to UAV's, no project you are working on is too small to talk about and share.

If you have any questions then please join our community on Google+ and then ask away.
